Member Organizations

MAES | Latinos in Engineering and Science

MAES at MIT is a tight-knit community that focuses on the professional development of its individual members, offering them resources and opportunities for success.

LUCHA

LUCHA at MIT is dedicated to provinding both cultural and academic support to students who identify in any way with the Latino culture. We are a familia dedicated to supporting each other and creating bonds that last throught a student's entire time here at MIT.

Casino Rueda

MIT Casino Rueda is a group devoted to promote, teach, and perform Rueda de Casino.

SHPE | Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers

SHPE is the Source for Quality Hispanic Engineers and Technical Talent.

Mujeres

Mujeres Latinas is an organization that seeks the social and professional advancement as well as the cultural enrichment of Latinas in the MIT community, promotes the success of individual Latinas by providing support and guidance in their endeavors, and... (?)
